Tips for Your Pet

Foods a Dog Should Eat

  • High-quality dog food (with balanced nutrients)
  • Lean meats (chicken, turkey, etc.)
  • Fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Fruits and vegetables (like apples, carrots, and green beans)

Best Places for a Dog

  • Spacious homes with room to run
  • Backyards or parks where they can exercise.
  • Dog-friendly cities or places with pet-friendly amenities

Best Environment for a Dog

  • Cold-weather dogs (e.g., Huskies, Saint Bernards) may prefer cooler environments
  • Warm-weather dogs (e.g., Greyhounds, Chihuahuas) prefer mild climates
